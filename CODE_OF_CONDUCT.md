# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, direct, and technically honest.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss ideas and implementations, not people
- assume good intent until evidence suggests otherwise
- give concrete feedback with enough detail to act on it
- keep disagreements technical and specific

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate derailment, spam, or bad-faith participation

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, follow the guidance in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) and [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) as appropriate.

Maintainer actions may include comment removal, thread locking, rejection of contributions, or repository bans.
